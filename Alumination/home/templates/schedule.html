COMING FULL CIRCLE

Coming Full Circle(CFC) witnesses an extraordinary alumnus coming back to the institute and giving insights to the audience, thus enlightening them with his knowledge.
SARC proudly announces the CFC guest for this year- Mr. Rizwan Koita, Co-founder and CEO of CitiusTech. He also co-founded TransWorks Information Services (now Minacs), one of India’s first 3rd party BPO organizations. Recipient of the prestigious 2013 Ernst & Young Entrepreneur Award and the ‘Young Achievers Award in Business’ in 2002, he holds an MSc degree in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science from MIT, Cambridge and Bachelors in Electrical Engineering from IIT Bombay.

GROUP MENTORING

“All it takes is a spark, and then shall spread the inferno.” Alumination brings you ‘Group Mentoring’ — a session to absorb and assimilate the key aspects of your field of interest with other equally driven and curious minds by your side. Having gathered along with your mentor, the discussion thereon aims to assist you in your decision-making process in the fields of analytics, consulting and finance. While the mentor, an expert in his respective domain, shares his insights on the sector in concern, the mentees can also discuss their simpler, more relatable viewpoints.
